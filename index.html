ADIT
(Networking & Cloud Computing)
NSTI NOIDA
Classroom Training

In this Diploma in IT, Networking and Cloud, the trainee is trained on Five Core modules each of
320 hours duration in the first year. Each Core module contains professional skills &
professional knowledge. In addition to this, the trainee is entrusted with project work
and extracurricular activities to build up confidence. In the second year, there are three electives
where the trainee has to select any of two elective modules, each module containing 320 hours
duration with a total duration of 640 hours. The trainee will be trained in Industry for 800 hours
(as a part of on-the-job training). There will be a common subject for all Diploma courses on Employability Skills which will be for 160 hours in the second year.
